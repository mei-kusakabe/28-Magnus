Our Inspiration


We are proud to be Indians. India is known for its varied Culture, Outstanding export business, Quality Education, Rich Heritage, One of the Fastest growing Economy, progressive in the fields of security, science, medicine, food grains, and the list can go on & on. India has managed to be among the top-6 economies in the world. India is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world. In spite of all this, we firmly believe that India and its citizens have a potential to grow much more in terms of Education, Skills and Wealth.

And that is Why Whatever we are now is no Enough. One percentage of rich population in India holds 20% of the country's wealth while 50% has 13% of wealth. “Over 20 crore people who live here are under the below poverty line. Around 23 crore people have an income of ₹375 per day. Unemployment rate is also very distressing as the current rate is 7.6%,”

Which means there has to be some plans that can do something about this economic inequality. Our education system is primarily focused only on examinations rather than training students for the future and really testing their knowledge. Because of this, students are forced to take tests that show only their retention powers, not their actual capacity or knowledge.

Our Beloved Prime Minister Honourable Shri Narendaji Modi in his addressing on World Youth’s Skill Day said, “The skill development of the new generation is a national need”. And going further to this 1 step, he kept the foundation of Aatmanirbhar Bharat

“While education tells us what to do, skill guides us in the actual operational implementation and this has been the guiding principle of Skill India Mission. So far more than 1.25 crore young persons have been trained under ‘Pradhanmantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana’,” said PM Modi. He Further said that “Learning should not stop with earning. Only a skilled person will grow in today’s world. This is applicable to both people and countries.” He said that India providing smart and skilled man-power solutions to the world should be at the core of our strategy of skilling our youth.
